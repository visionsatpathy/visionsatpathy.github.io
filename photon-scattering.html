Photon Scattering Reconstruction

Scattering is often treated as noise. In reality, it encodes depth limits, phase distortions, and SNR variations.

This project explores forward scattering models, reconstruction stability, and depth extension strategies under realistic photon transport assumptions.

Future additions:

  • Forward model simulations
  • Depth vs scattering coefficient analysis
  • Phase distortion mapping
  • Comparative inversion strategies
